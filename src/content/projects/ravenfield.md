**JLA Architects · Brighton, CO · For the Brighton Housing Authority · In design**

Ravenfield is designed to integrate into its surrounding residential neighborhood
while fostering connection and community among its senior residents. Amenities
include a community room, library, business center, coffee bar, rooftop terrace,
and direct access to walking paths.

I contributed during schematic design, participating in client meetings and
facilitating a community design charrette with neighboring residents. Using
scaled site elements, I gathered feedback that directly informed the project's
site organization and building placement.
