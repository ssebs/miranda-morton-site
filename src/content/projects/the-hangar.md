**JLA Architects · Central Park, Denver, CO · Under construction for Clear Sky Capital**

The Hangar replaces a former airport hangar on one of the last remaining parcels
in North America's largest master-planned community. The four-story wrap building
draws on a "Nordic Chic" aesthetic, with a muted material palette anchored by
fiber cement, brick, glulam timber, and cable railings.

I independently produced the project master plan and contributed across most
phases from schematic design through construction documentation — developing
floor plans, reflected ceiling plans, exterior elevations, interior elevations,
wall sections, and enlarged unit plans, with particular attention to
accessibility compliance and building envelope coordination.
