**Design Studio 4 · M.Arch, University of Colorado Denver · 1,543 SF · Morrison, CO**

The design centers on a rotunda capped by an octagonal cupola and square widow's
walk, which brings natural light down into the heart of the plan. Wings extend
from the rotunda to accommodate dining and kitchen on one side and a great room
on the other, while a rear wing contains the primary suite and a guest bedroom.
A formal parterre garden extends the building's axis into the landscape.

Proportional analysis — including golden ratio studies applied to the elevation
and plan — governed the sizing and placement of structural bays, windows, and
classical details. Column capital studies and trim details were developed at
large scale, demonstrating close engagement with the classical orders. Produced
in AutoCAD, Revit, Twinmotion, and Photoshop.
